## Publication guardrails (ENFORCED)

✅ **ALLOWED**:
- Testimonial text exactly as published on dcroofing.ca/about-us/testimonials/
- Reviewer names/initials as published on the source page
- Attribution: "Published on dcroofing.ca" with link to source
- No invented quotes, ratings, or reviewer names
- No customer photos (unless explicitly permitted by source)

❌ **NOT ALLOWED**:
- Invented testimonials or fabricated reviews
- Paraphrasing or editing published text
- Customer photos without clear reuse permission
- Claiming reviews came from Google if they came from dcroofing.ca
- Creating a fake "reviews section" that implies the concept owner collected them

## Implementation Plan

The reviews section will:
1. Display 5–6 genuine testimonials from dcroofing.ca/about-us/testimonials/
2. Show reviewer name/attribution exactly as published
3. Include a clear label: "Published on dcroofing.ca"
4. Link to the source page for full context
5. Use text-only (no customer photos)
6. Be positioned between the About section and Contact section
7. Follow Pacific Shield Modernism design system (storm-charcoal bg, mineral text, lichen accents)
